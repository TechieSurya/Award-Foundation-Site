Dharsana Foundation is a nongovernmental, non-profit making voluntary social service organization established in 2023 under Indian Charitable Trusts Act of 1882. It is also registered as a charity under the Income Tax act 80G, 12 A(a)Giving back to the community is an essential part of the Dharsana Foundation's mission. Dharsana's commitment to giving has been demonstrated through its 5+ years of supporting and rewarding students. We are now expanding in giving and have become a registered Foundation, so we can support even more people and causes as we go forward.

More than 2000 individuals have been recognized for their deprived status, then given hope, and support, and they excelled in their careers.

As part of this tradition, a number of events are held every year in order to collect funds for deserving students and youths. These events are held so that they can be recognized and encouraged for their efforts.
